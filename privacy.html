Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy outlines how MoodSphere collects, uses, and protects your data.

Information We Collect

  • Mood entries you submit
  • Time of mood entries (for reminders)

How We Use Your Information

All data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. We do not collect, transmit, or store your data on external servers.

Your Control

You can clear your mood history at any time. Data does not leave your device.

Security

Since no external storage is used, your data remains secure on your device unless manually deleted.

For questions, please contact us at support@moodsphere.com
