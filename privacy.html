Privacy Policy

At MoodSphere, your privacy is our top priority. This policy explains how we handle your personal information when you use our mood tracking application.

Information We Collect

MoodSphere is designed with privacy in mind:

  • Your mood entries and personal notes are stored locally on your device
  • We do not collect or transmit your personal mood data to external servers
  • No account registration or personal information is required
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies

Local Data Storage

Your data stays on your device

All your mood tracking data, including:

  • Daily mood selections
  • Personal notes and reflections
  • Reminder preferences
  • Theme settings

Are stored locally in your browser's storage and never transmitted to our servers or third parties.

Data Security

Since your data remains on your device:

  • You have complete control over your information
  • Data is not vulnerable to external data breaches
  • Clearing your browser data will remove all stored information
  • No third parties have access to your personal mood data

Notifications

If you choose to enable reminder notifications:

  • We only use your browser's built-in notification system
  • No personal data is included in notifications
  • You can disable notifications at any time through your browser settings

Important: Since data is stored locally, backing up your browser data is recommended if you want to preserve your mood tracking history.

Changes to Privacy Policy

Any updates to this privacy policy will be reflected on this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
